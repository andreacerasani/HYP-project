Trevino conducts Mahler


    The Symphony No. 7 by Gustav Mahler was written in 1904-05, with repeated revisions to the scoring. It is sometimes referred to by the title Song of the Night (German: Lied der Nacht), which Mahler never knew. The Symphony no. 7 is among the most difficult and complex of Mahler's nine symphonies, according to the composer himself, who worked on it in a fit of rage in almost total isolation. The Symphony was less successful with the public, compared to the remarkable interest in the music scene. Robert Trevino (born 1984) is an American conductor. He is currently music director of the Basque National Orchestra in Spain, principal guest conductor of the Orchestra Sinfonica Nazionale della RAI, and artistic advisor of the Malmo Symphony Orchestra in Sweden.
