Must Sees of Venice

    One cannot pass through Venice without visiting these places. This itinerary will take you to the city's landmarks, those who made Venice an unforgettable place, from St. Mark's Basilica to the Doge's Palace. This journey through Venetian culture will make even the shortest of stays in Venice an experience to remember for life
