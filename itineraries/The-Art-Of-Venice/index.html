The Art Of Venice

    The itinerary allows you to learn about the greatest artists who worked in the city of Venice, let us guide you on a journey through art and culture. Starting from the Gallerie dell'Accademia to the Biennale you will discover works and stories that will remain in your heart forever.

    The Gallerie dell'Accademia is a museum gallery of pre-19th-century art in Venice, northern Italy. It is housed in the Scuola della Carità on the south bank of the Grand Canal, within the sestiere of Dorsoduro.
